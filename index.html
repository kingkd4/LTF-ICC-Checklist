Body close only. A wick through does NOT count. Must be a full candle close.

Correction

Watch for internal structure shift
The correction is losing steam and price is starting to shift back in the direction of the trend. This is your signal that the pullback may be ending.
Invalidation
Did structure break the WRONG way?
If price breaks below a key swing (bullish setup) = correction failed. Walk away. Wait for new setup.
The correction is not complete at the pullback. It is complete at the RETEST + CLOSE.

The Golden Rule — Candle Closure Confirmation

Every phase of ICC must be confirmed with a candle closure through the Pre-Indication Level. A wick does not count. Price must fully close through the level.

Phase 1 — A 1M candle closes through the Pre-Indication Level. This confirms the Indication.
Phase 2 — Price pulls back and retests the Pre-Indication Level. The retest confirms this is a valid ICC setup.
Phase 3 — A 1M candle closes back through the level in the direction of the trend. This is your entry trigger.
No candle closure = no confirmation = no trade. Every time.
